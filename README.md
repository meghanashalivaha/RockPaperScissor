How to Play:
1.Open the game in your browser
2.Click one of the buttons:
3.Rock
4.Paper
5.Scissors
6.The computer will randomly choose its move
7.The winner of the round will be displayed
8.Scores will update automatically
9.First to reach 5 points wins the game
10.Click "Reset Game" to play again

Game Rules:
1.Rock beats Scissors
2.Scissors beats Paper
3.Paper beats Rock
4.Same choice results in Draw
